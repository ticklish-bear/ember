Privacy Policy

Ember — Cycle Chart & NFP Tracker  •  Last updated: 24 June 2026

Ember is designed to be private by default. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data storage

All data you enter — cycle days, temperatures, mucus observations, notes — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is transmitted to any server, and no account is required to use the app.

Data we collect

We collect nothing. Ember contains no analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking of any kind. The app has no internet permission and makes no network requests.

Third-party services

Ember uses no third-party services. There are no embedded SDKs from Google, Meta, or any other company that would receive your data.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all data from your device. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.

Children

Ember is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If the privacy policy changes in a material way (for example, if optional cloud backup is added in a future version), the updated policy will be published at this URL and the “last updated” date above will change.
