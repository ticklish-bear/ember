Ember is a charting app for the symptothermal method (STM, also known as NFP/FAM). It's a digital chart — built to support how you already work, not to replace your judgement with a black box.

WHAT IT IS
Ember records the signs you track: basal body temperature, cervical mucus, cervix, bleeding, and notes. The evaluation follows the published symptothermal rules and the AG NFP guidelines — nothing else.

• No algorithm that "learns" from your data
• No predictions guessed from past cycle lengths
• No proprietary logic
If a day is marked fertile, you can trace exactly which rule caused it.

PRIVACY FIRST
• Everything is stored locally on your device
• No account, no sign-in, no cloud
• No ads, no analytics, no trackers
• Works fully offline — nothing leaves your phone

LEARN THE METHOD
Ember includes an extensive section on the biology behind the method: the hormonal cycle, why the biomarkers work, and how the rules follow from the underlying physiology. Ideally you learn STM from a qualified instructor — this is here so you can understand the method properly rather than follow rules on faith.

FREE
The full app is free for everyone. If you'd like to support development you can leave an optional tip, but it unlocks nothing — everyone gets the complete app.

IMPORTANT
Ember is a charting tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and is not a substitute for a qualified instructor. The symptothermal method is effective when applied correctly by someone trained in it. If you use STM to avoid pregnancy, learn the method properly before relying on it.
